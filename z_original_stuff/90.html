Dearly Beloved in Christ,

Greetings in the name of our Lord and Savior Jesus Christ. 

I hope all of you had a wonderful Summer Break. The schools have reopened after the long vacation. We have sent our children to school with lots of hopes and dreams. The Children also getting ready to begin one more academic year .I pray and wish for all our school and college going students and wish them every success in this year. The Bible says that, ”The fear of the Lord is the beginning  of knowledge ,but fools despise  wisdom and discipline.”(Prov:1:7) 

Education is not only for imparting some knowledge , but also for bringing a person out into the world as a good citizen .It is to impart good qualities in human beings. These days the children have getting good school ,books ,dresses ,food, car and all other best facilities. But some students are fascinated by the widespread mass media culture and received it as a model. They are failing to behave or appear properly in our family and society. Our children should be encouraged to search for the true wisdom that is found in following the footsteps of the Lord. It will make them understand that life is worth living, when lived with the Lord. That is the true purpose of education. 

This is the suitable time to tell our children about the situation that our parents suffered even to have our basic education. The students have a great responsibility to understand the mind and dreams of the parents. Learning is not to the collection of some information only,but also it should be a great treasure of love and care of the family and the society. 

The month of September all Malayalies are celebrating the festival of ONAM. It is a great memory of love and prosperity and equality. . I hope that day will be a remembrance of our native food dress and culture. 

WISH YOU A GREAT AND HAPPY ONAM 

God Bless You all

Yours in His Service

Rev Benjamin George
