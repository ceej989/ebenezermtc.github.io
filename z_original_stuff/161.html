                                       CHECK YOUR EXPECTATION LEVEL

   

Dearly beloved in Christ,

                   We are entering the second month of 2014 Let us thank God for His abundant mercy showered upon us. God’s love is good beyond comprehension. It repairs the damage caused by our sins and errors. It makes whole our personal stories after they have been broken by sin; it heals all of history.

               

                 Discouragement is the result of a confusion of expectations_ more often than not, the result of unrealistic thinking. Did you expect every one to be honest and truthful? Did you really see the world as free from all human jealousy, sin, and evil? Did you really think anyone is always respected by everyone? Does everyone respect any person who has accomplished something? Hardly! Bad people do not respect the work of good people. Negative people do not respect positive people.

                 We must close the doors to the jealousies, envy and gossip that divide and destroy our communities. Rumors are the weapons of the devil.

                 Determine today that you are going to be a positive person even if everyone doesn’t believe you. Decide to be a kind, honest and generous person. Yes you may loose the support of some negative persons. Let them go. Pay the price. After all, they probably aren’t the people that you respect the most. Let us try to become a positive – thinking person. And when good people see you coming, they announce joyfully, “Here comes Good News!’’

                                                                          MAY GOD BLESS US ALL.

                                                                               SUJAN ACHEN
