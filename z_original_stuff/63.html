My Beloved in Christ, 


 The month of April we celebrated the Easter festival.Our Lord and Savior. Jesus Christ is risen! Praise the Lord. We are the Ambassadors of this Christ. Our duty is to convey this gospel .This is a message of Hope. So we have to exchange this truth to generations to generation.Jesus' Voice call us to do this duty in every day. We have only one life to live in this world. So we have to live for Christ. If we do so we could speak and live for our Jesus. Ambassador is a responsible and faithful duty. Are we faith full to  God. How we work for God.?If we work for men we cannot please god. If we try to please the society, we cannot give to others the gospel of hope. Jesus showed that model. Through the Church this truth would have reveal. Let us pray for the spiritual growth of our church and the upcoming program like V.B.S., Church convention,etc.

Thank you. 

Yours in His Service 

Yours Benjaminachen.
