VICAR’S MESSAGE

Greetings to you all in the precious name of our Lord and Savior Jesus Christ.

I am happy to learn that many of our parish members are observing lent. During this Lenten period let us re-dedicate our life in the hands of Christ and regain strength to proclaim the good news to all. According to Gustavo Gutierrez evangelizing means “communicating the joy of knowing that we are loved by God, the joy of knowing that life is the ultimate word in human existence.” A new life is beginning with the resurrection of the Lord. All the four evangelists record the triumphant fact that on the third day Jesus rose again from the dead. The four gospels tell us that the women were the first persons who went to see the tomb. By seeing the empty tomb and the risen Lord they have experienced in the most direct way. The empty tomb is the sign of a new presence to which they are to bear witness. Mark gives the particular timing when the women went to the tomb. “Very early on the first day of the week, just after sunrise they were on there way to the tomb” (Mark 16:2). The focus is on the idea of beginning, a new start. Baptism is a sign, an effective sign in the Church. Its significant is our bound with Christ. In the death of Christ, the power of sin, of self sufficient egoism dies. We have been baptized into death (Rom.6:3). Paul reinforces the idea of resurrection, adding that “we were therefore buried with him through baptism into death” (v.4). Because of that, just as Christ was raised, we too have been called to walk “in the newness of life” (v.4). As we are celebrating the Easter, let us have a new start and a newness of life.

March 2, Sunday is observing as Diocesan Sunday. A joint World Day of Prayer meeting of Ebenezer, St Andrews, St Thomas and Hudson valley CSI church will be held on Mar 8 at 9.30am at St Thomas. We have Holy Communion on Hosanna, on Easter, and Good Friday Worship during Holy week. I request all your prayerful participation. This month we have Regional meetings of sevika sanghom(Mar 1 ,at our parish) ,Yuvajana sakhyam (Mar.15, at St James),Youth Fellowship(Mar.22)and Zonal Savika Sanghom General Body on Mar.9 at 3 pm at St Andrews. Yuvajana Sakhyam Platinum Jubilee Valedictory meeting will be held at Staten Island Church on Mar.29 at 3pm.Please make it convenient to participate in these meetings and be blessed.

May these services may be a blessing to all of us.

Yours in Christ’s service

Johnson Atchen.
