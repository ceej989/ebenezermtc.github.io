Dearly Beloved in Christ,

Greetings in the name of our Lord and Savior Jesus Christ.

“I have the Strength to face all conditions by the power that Christ gives me.”(Phil: 4:13)

During great Lent season, our meditation is upon the transforming Christ.  Jesus transforms in different ways, both spiritually, and physically.  The transforming Christ restores and strengthens us.  Jesus Christ died on the cross not only for our sins, but also to give us abundant life.

A Word of Thanks………

I am grateful to God for calling me to serve in New York Ebenezer Mar Thoma Church and St. Andrews Mar Thoma Church, for last three years.  With gratitude, I remember the fellowship I have received from all members from these parishes.  It was an enriching experience for me to work here.  I would like to take this time to thank all members of Ebenezer Mar Thoma Parish for the fellowship, care, and concern extended to me and my family.  Special appreciation goes to all office bearers who served the parish, during my tenure for their committed and wholehearted involvement in the day to day administration of the parish. We shall ever grateful to you for your love, prayer, and concern. Your love to my children, Kripa and Madhu, are appreciated.  We are planning to leave on 22nd of April to Kerala and to join at Chennai (Madras) on May 4th.  Kindly uphold us in your prayers. Thank you so much for everything.  Once again we express our gratitude to all parish members.

God Bless You.

Yours Benjaminachen.
