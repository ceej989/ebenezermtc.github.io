My Beloved in Christ,

Greetings in the name of our Lord and savior Jesus Christ.

God is calling every one of us .He wants to talk to everyone of us to make us his tools to witness the resurrected Christ to others, to the world, to the society around us. God called Samuel, when he was a child, but he was too young to recognize that it was God who had called him. We too may be infants in our faith, in spiritual growth; through we may be parading as the preachers in our church. Side by side we may be hearing the call of the world. We must first discern the call and identify whose call it is before answering. There are verity of calls of God to different persons according to the talents each one possesses for some people it is a call to leadership, for some others it is a call to be ministers, and likewise to each one of us according to the talents each one  possesses for the glory of God.

May God grant us the spirit to listen to his call and to answer positively to be a witness to his calling.

Our children are coming to the end of one more academic year; My God pour His abundant grace to both who is attending the examination and whose exams are over equally.

By the Grace of God we got three RAMBANS, Very Rev Varghese Mathai, Very Rev Ommen George and Very Rev K.V.Varkey, Let us congratulate and pray for them.

May God Bless You

Rev Benchamen George
