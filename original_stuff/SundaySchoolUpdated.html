April 3, 2013

 

Re: Diocesan Sunday SchoolSilver Jubilee Project

 

Dear Vicars, Superintendents, Sunday School Teachers, Parents and Children,

 

We have been blessed as a Diocese for the past 25 years. Similarly, our Sunday School has grown to 56 participating Sunday Schools and over 3000 children.  Even though we are a settled community in this land, for most of our children this is their land where they are born. We are blessed by many measures.  Just as the Israelites were led to the land of ‘milk and honey’, we have been brought to this land of great resources and opportunities.

 

With that in mind, it is important that we instill in our children that the blessings that we have are not due to our own merit but because of the grace of our loving God.  It is our responsibility as children of God to share these gifts with those who are less fortunate than us.

 

As part of the Diocesan Silver Jubilee Celebrations, the Diocesan Sunday School has decided to mark the jubilee year by instituting the “Kids Helping Kids” project.  The goal of the project is to provide scholarships for higher education for underprivileged children in India.  It is our Diocesan Bishop’s vision to use this celebration to share our blessings with those who would not otherwise have the same opportunities as our own children.

 

We are asking the entire parish to join with the local Sunday School to raise funds for this worthy cause.  Please remit all funds to the Diocesan Sunday School Treasurer, Dr. John K. Thomas by June 21, 2013. 

 

Let this be a time of celebration and introspection.  The Sunday School lessons taught in the classroom need to be applied in the real world.  Our ministry needs to go beyond our Sunday school and the four walls of our church.  Let us not forget that all children are gifts from God.

 

Thank you in advance for your cooperation and support.  Please keep our Diocesan Bishop, Sunday School and the entire diocese in your prayers.  May God continue to bless us and strengthen us.

 

In Christ,

Tom Philip

Secretary
