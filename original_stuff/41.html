Vicar's message

Greetings to you all in the precious name of our Lord and Savior Jesus Christ.

In this month we are celebrating Easter, the resurrection of our Lord Jesus Christ. Resurrection of our Lord has made great changes in the world. Before resurrection death was the last word and the end but after resurrection we have the promise of everlasting life and it changes the whole concept of death itself. Beyond death we have eternal life, it is not the end of everything but it opens us a new era of life. Before Christ’s resurrection cross was known only as an instrument of horrible death, and a symbol of curse. But it was transformed as a symbol of blessing after his resurrection. Resurrection gives us hope in our desperate situations. Let us have hope in God even in the lifeless situations.

This may be the last letter through which I am addressing you. We came over here on May 9, 2006 and leaving on April 27, 2009. We really enjoyed the ministry over here. We are really thankful to you all for your support, care and cooperation. We also express our sincere thanks to the office bears from2006-09, Executive committee members and office bears of the organizations for their sincere work and support. I love our Sunday school children, I like to be with the youths, I enjoy the fellowship of Yuvajana Sakhyam members and I like to hear the melodious singing of the choir, I will always remember the care and concern of the Sevika Sanghom and Edavaka Mission Members. I am appointed as the Director of “Malabar Mar Thoma Mission Complex” which is located at Uppada. There we have an Old Age Home, Child Care Project, De-Addiction Centre. Kindly do pray for the mission work over there and support financially.

THANK YOU & GOD BLESS.
WISHING YOU ALL A HAPPY EASTER

Yours in Christ’s Sevice.
Rev Johnson C. Jacob
