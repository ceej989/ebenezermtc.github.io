“Sing praise to the Lord, because he has done great things. Let all the world know what he has done."

My dear parish members

Greetings to all of you in the name of our Savior Jesus Christ,

Christmas is over, the declarations and celebrations are coming down. We have celebrated the birth of Jesus with carols, special services, and family get togethers. This was the remembrance of the amazing culmination of the promise of God. If we couldn’t give proper importance for Jesus among our parties, we have to rethink our spiritual dimensions. In the New Year there should be a radical change in our mind and day to day lives. Let us examine whether the precepts and teachings of God are preserved in our mind and heart. As we enter in to a new year It is the time we opened our hearts before God .Let us try to renew ourselves. When we live according to the plan and purpose of God, we will enjoy the blessings of the Lord and will be a blessing to others.

May The God almighty shower His Blessings for a happy healthy and spiritual New Year and May His Blessing continue to be showered on all of us throughout the New Year.

HAPPY NEW YEAR

Yours Benjaminachen
