My Beloved parish members in Christ, 

Praise the Lord in the name of our resurrected Lord and savior Jesus Christ.

Resurrection of the dead is the key stone in the arch of the Christian faith. The entire Gospel rests on the fact of Christ's resurrection. It is the basis of the hope of the believer's resurrection. Christ's resurrection represents the greatest victory and the most glorious fact of the eternity. It is the crucifixion of the son of God was earth’s darkest hour, the morning of his resurrection was eternity’s brightest. The empty tomb of the Christ is the greatest recorded miracle of the Bible, and the greatest known miracle of the universe. It is the abiding miracle of the eternity. So today Jesus speaks to us the Greatest Commission authority in heaven and on earth has been given to me. Go therefore and make disciples of all nations…I am with you always to the close of the age. “Mathew. 28:18-20 Go into all the world and preach the Gospel to the whole creation.”

Now the resurrected Christ speaks to us go to the world and help the creation to enter in to the kingdom of God. “The harvest is plentiful, but the laborers are few.”

The VBS will be conducted on July. So please pray for that and the Sunday school examination will be on 15th May. Parents please try to send all children to attend both programs, I hope all members will visit and the VBS. Let us remember on our family prayer and in the personal prayer for the purchasing of the new land.

May God Bless You All  

Yours in His Service

Yours Benchamen George
