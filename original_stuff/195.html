Dearly Beloved In Christ,

Greetings to you in the name of our Lord and Savior Jesus Christ. We are greatly blessed to live in the land and enjoy all the benefits of this country. Let us enjoy all the benefits of this country. Let us thanks before the Almighty with humble hearts.

 

We are entering Christmas time- what is Christmas?. Christmas is making room for the action of God. Christmas a feast of contradictions. In Luke: 2:4-7 we see the Biblical narrative- what is the message contained in the story? What meaning can it have for us today? God's relationship to his creation- God our liberator comes to us in total poverty. Yet at the same time, his birth is greeted by angels and shepherds, wise man and a star. The creator of all accepts to be laid in a manger.

 

Christmas should be a moment of beginning a new of renewal of return to the roofs of our faith. Christmas is a feast of commitment. God comes to us to free us from all that keeps us in bondage. Christ brings us liberation in new and unexpected ways. Through the incarnation, death and resurrection of our redeemer, we are freed from the power of our redeemer; we are freed from the power of death and called to a new life.

 

The image and likeness of God are restored in us. What greater love and commitment could God show towards us? This very fact calls us to make the same commitment. The incarnation challenges us to keep contemplation and action in a dynamic creative tension. After all, this is the feast of the union of things, which seem apparently contradictory: the creator becomes a creature, the virgin becomes a mother, heaven and earth are united. The invisible becomes visible. Here there is a total reversal of values. Christmas is a time of hope and joy.

           ☺ WISH YOU A MERRY MERRY HAPPY CHRISTMAS☺

                                                               May God Bless Us All

                                                                                                                                            Yours In Christ

                                                                                                                                            Sujan Achen
