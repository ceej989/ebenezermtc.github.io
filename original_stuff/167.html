                                                                         Our Self – Image

Dearly beloved in Christ,              

                                          The human mind was the first computer ever created and will remain forever the brightest and the best. Erik Erikson, father of child psychiatry, has taught the world that; the first thing a child must learn is trust. The clinging is a demonstration of a lack of trust and inborn insecurity, a first flattering step on what will be a lifetime of learning to achieve self- confidence. So Erikson has taught the nurses to stroke and speak tenderly to the newborn babies, to program them to trust. This hoped for emerging trust will be a fragile development. If the two-year-old “computer” is programmed with fear teachings, his / her developing trust level will be impaired. If the child is abused by his / her father, the “computer” will record this negative experience. Later if that developing teenaged mind is introduced to the concept of God as a heavenly Father, the “computer” will light up the screen with a flash alarm, warning Reject!  Reject!. Atheism or agnosticism may be programmed by the computer in to the subconscious mind, predetermining what may well be a lifetime, Unbelief- Belief system.

 

                        Good News and Bad News – this incredible computer, the subconscious,

will keep on being programmed as long as there is brain life. If at the first grade level the

mind is programmed to believe that God is a mean, unloving, super-spirit –power, then

we should not be surprised if the child that was reared in this negative, legalistic,religious

atmosphere grows up to react and reject the entire concept of God or he may reflect

an angry religious lifestyle. Even as the “computer” programming will largely determine

the religious belief system, so it’s programming determine the self-image. If the parent

shouts in anger “you are stupid’ the computer will believe this. It does not debate. It merely

records the lie garbage in_ garbage out. The mind is set to forecast predictions such as “I

am not smart enough to get an A.’ I am not college material’. The self-image is evolving

in the computer. The computer never experiences a “Power Shutdown”. A human life is

on the way to developing a negative self- image which will generate low self- esteem,

which will lead to failure, which will reinforce the negative self- image, which will

become a self- fulfilling expectation!

 

Our self – image tends to become a self – fulfilling prophecy. So developing a positive

Self-image is of primary importance. Without it, the hoped for healthy self respect is a

lost cause. Helplessness, perceived or real, leads to a sense of hopelessness in the mind of

a person with a negative self –image. That’s a prescription for defeat, discouragement,

depression and despair. The negative self image infection in society that feeds and fuels

an epidemic of low self- esteem. Rejected, Discouraged, Disappointed with the results,

Divorced and bankrupt are thoughts of negative self – image people and these keep them

down .It is important to recognize that the image of God that’s engraved in our

subconscious will shape our self – image and in turn affect the state of your self- esteem.

                                                                                         May God Bless Us All

 

HAPPY EASTER

Lovingly    

Sujan achen

                   

EASTER MESSAGE

I Extend Easter   Greetings in the matchless name of the Risen, Immortal and Adorable name of the Lord and Saviour Jesus Christ, the authority of ‘Life and Death’ and ‘Heaven and Earth’

  His resurrection is seen as the turning point of human history. It is the cornerstone of Christian life. It is a history-making, earth-shaking, life-transforming, and eternity-changing truth - a truth that changes human faith, theology, and life.

 Easter is the staple of our faith and it’s the resurrection that gives us hope. The resurrection of Jesus from the dead allows believers to place their faith in Jesus Christ to have a guarantee of eternal life. And He offers us hope in the midst of what’s happening in our lives in the world today and all we need to do is turn to him .

May Lord bless you on this auspicious day of Easter, and May it be a new beginning of greater prosperity, success and happiness. May Jesus bless you immensely on this auspicious occasion.  May you have a pleasant Easter.

With love & Prayers

SUJAN ACHEN

Beloved in Christ,

 

We are on the verge of celebrating Easter- The festival of hope. The spirit of Easter is the spirit of hope. Resurrection of Jesus Christ is the foundation of Christian faith. Easter experience is a proof of our own resurrection. The word of God, in Romans 8:11,says  “And if the spirit of Him who raised Jesus from the dead is living in you, He who raised Christ from the dead will also give life to your mortal bodies because of His spirit who lives in you.” We therefore are sacramental beings-set apart for God to be with him. Easter gives us this ultimate assurance.

May the Easter experience fill our hearts to lead a life filled with hope and joy.

Wishing you a happy Easter.

God bless you. 

 

SUJAN ACHEN
