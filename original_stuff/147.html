Let your word be ‘Yes’ ‘yes’ and ‘No’ ‘No’.

 

Dearly Beloved in Christ,

              Every human being has the awareness of the presence of God in his or her conscience. Thomas Carlyle says, Make yourself an honest man and then you may be sure there is one reseal hers in the world. Take for instance our children in our families, they do what is not right in the absence of their parents when the parents catch them, the immediate answer is “No, I did not do it, so and so did it. Not all the children are dishonest all the time.

There can be many people who protest against corruption but unless and until each individual makes an effort to be sincere and truthful in his or her daily life no amount of protests and fasts can change the condition of our social life-Are we sincere and truthful in our daily life?

How often all of us say ‘Yes” for ‘No’ and No for ‘Yes’? We know well that we are doing something wrong, but still we continue doing it just the same.  Day in and day out life is mingled with thoughts, words and deeds that are not going together. This attitude of incoherence could be due to our surrounding and distorted values that we imbibe from our neighbors and friends. The economic differences are great, the rich grows richer and richer while the poor man tumbles down from poverty to misery. The prices of everything have sky rocketed in the last few years. Jesus Christ in his sermons says that He is the way, the truth and the life. No man could utter such words and calls himself as truth. All of us as poor limited human beings should try our best to be very truthful and sincere with god who can see our in most thoughts. To ourselves through self knowledge and honest reflections- and with every person we come in contact so that we may imitate our Divine Master and model who is truth itself.

“We should always speak the truth but not all the truths should be spoken.”

                                                                                       May God Bless Us All,

                                                                                                                      Sujan Achen
