"And I will always guide you and satisfy you with good things. I will keep you strong and well. You will be a garden that has plenty of water, like a spring of water that never runs dry".

                                                         -  Isaiah 58:11

  

My Beloved Parish Members,

Once again God has blessed us with the privilege to enter a New Year!  Our life and prosperity is the gift of God.  See how great our Savior is!  How great is His protection and guidance!  Do we see the glory of God?  We have to see the glory of God in our day to day life.  If we fail to recognize that, we fail to accept Jesus Christ as our Personal Savior.  In this New Year we need to come back to strong faith, love and dedication.  This is the time to re-dedicate ourselves to Him and beg for His pardon.  Our Lord and Savior Jesus Christ, renews and sustains us.  When we truly understand Him we will be more faithful, loyal and dedicated to His call and will. As we start to walk into the new hours, weeks, months, years, let us look back with a thankful heart and remember His blessings which have been showered upon us.

 

I WISH YOU A HAPPY NEW YEAR!

May God bless you all!

 

 Yours In His Service,

 Benjaminachen
