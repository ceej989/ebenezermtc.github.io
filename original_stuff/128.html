Do something

 

Dear achens and parish members

The North East Regional Youth Fellowship is taking a new venture to spend one day in the city once in a month with the Homeless people.  NERYF has been working hand in hand with the youth of our region and the New York Rescue Mission, in creating an event to show the love of Christ outside of our community. The first mission activity will be held on Sunday June 15th, 2014, at 12:30 PM running through the early evening ending at 7:00 PM. Our project will be divided into four segments: cooking and serving, hygiene, praise and worship and medical assistance. We hope that through our service, we will be able to spread the word and serve His kingdom. We request your prayers and support for the successful competition of this project. The dates of the following months will be informed later.

 

Yours in His ministry

 

Devin Abraham

Secretary

 

Rev. V. M. Mathew

President




April 25, 2014

Re: North East Regional Youth Fellowship (NERYF) Missions Event

The NERYF executive committee would like to sincerely extend our thanks for

signing up and taking the first step in this monthly mission’s activity. As the team briefly

mentioned during this past Spring One Day conference, NERYF is teaming up with NY

Rescue Mission to organize a monthly missions program in NYC. Our objective is to

serve and use this event as a channel to share the gospel.

Agenda: NY Rescue mission will be opening up their new facilities on May 9th-

10th, 2014. They have kindly given us the opening date of June 15th, 2014 from 12-9pm.

They have agreed to allot us this time and provide those whom we will be serving. The

missions work is broken down into the following four categories:

1.Food

2.Medical Check-Ups

3.Hygiene / Grooming Station

4.Praise and Worship

1. Food

We plan to Purchase, Prep, and Present food and drinks for each and every individual

who comes to the venue. We will need volunteers to help with providing / purchasing the

food needed, prepping the food, serving the food, and finally helping to clean up (group

effort)

2.Medical Check Up

We would like to have a team consisting of one doctor and several nurses and other

trained medical attendees. Our hope is to serve the population by providing them a

routine check up, similar to what we receive during our annual physicals.

3.Hygiene / Grooming Station

Although many of us have an abundance of grooming tools and unlimited showers at our

own individual homes, those who live on the streets are not privileged with this luxury.

We plan on giving the men and women haircuts, manicure, pedicures, washing their feet

and hands etc. We will assist them with daily grooming tasks that we often take for

granted.

4.Praise and Worship

With great food, top notch service, and medical staff at your fingertips, entertainment is

all that is missing. We would like to have a team of musicians and vocalists who will be

music to the ears of the less fortunate, peacefully soothing their minds, and opening their

hearts to God.

As depicted above, each mission’s event will be divided up into 4 parts. We ask that you

reply back with a confirmation letting us know what your talents are and whichfield you will be able to help us with. Please also spread the word and pray for this

initiative and that God be our focus so we can be Christ-like in everything we plan

and do.

Overview:

Who: NERYF Missions

What: Service

Where: NY Rescue HQ (NYC Rescue Mission 90 Lafayette Street New York, NY

10013-4494)

When: Sunday June 15th, 2014 @ 12:00 PM

Why: Because we were sent on this world to serve.

IHS,

NERYF E-Board
