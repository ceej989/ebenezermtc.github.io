Vicar’s Message Attitude Is What Counts

Dearly beloved in Christ,

         Three laborers were dragging massive stones. The first was asked by a reporter what he was doing. The replay was terse, “I am dragging a big stone and it’s breaking my back.”  He put the same query to the second fellow. His replay was, “I am working in this construction site to earn a living and I need your help right now.” The journalist politely declined. He moved on to ask the third man. He replied with a smile: “Sir, I am building a cathedral for God.” We call this attitude. In the four line story we have described three attitudes. Two important words are used in wellbeing and happiness domains, namely, choice and attitude. We have written about life being made up choices in most of our wellbeing articles and the topic of wellbeing and self will not be complete without writing about that all important word ‘attitude’. Life is definitely a combination of light and shade, happiness and sorrow, black and white, rain and sunshine. In any given situation some individuals cannot   just be put down. They always bounce back. They rise from the ashes. This is called attitude. It is said that “The greatest discovery of this generation is that human beings can alter their life by altering their attitude.”It is all in the mind. The day I control my thoughts and emotion I develop a positive attitude. Attitude can be defined as the way I look at life,failure, success, adversity, problems etc. Attitude is mainly seen in the way we define and practically live our life in terms of success and happiness. It is our attitude that determines our inner happiness and peace and it has an impact on the happiness and peace of those around us. It is contagious .We begin to posses the right attitude on the day weaccept responsibility for our own life. Olympic gold medalist Scott Hamilton once said, “The only disability in life is a bad attitude.” Nothing will hinder or handicap you in life as severely as a bad attitude. In the words of Joyce Meyer attitude refers to the system of thoughts, the mental posture, the mind- set, or the way of thinking with which a person approaches life. If my thoughts about my life or work are negative or bad naturally my life will turn out to be negative but  if my thoughts about my life,my job,my friends are positive then my life in turn becomes exciting. Life is ours to live and the right attitude can help us calm any storm.

MAY THE GOOD LORD BLESS US AND KEEP US ALL.

In His service - Sujan Achen
