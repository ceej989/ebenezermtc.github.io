Love or Loneliness- It’s your choice

Dearly Beloved,

Not to take the fun out of life. But to turn the Sun on in our life! They are designed to put pride back into our life and keep shame out.

One sizzling sentence helps us make the right choice between:

Fidelity-or philandering.

Integrity-or ambiguity.

Trust- or deception.

Pride- or shame.

Pleasure- or pain.

Love- or loneliness

These are real choices! This is how the options really stack up in real life!

We need wisdom to make the right decisions, when we are faced with the choices that carry such lasting consequences. Some choices are so important that wrong selections leave sears that can’t be covered with fresh paint. Remodeling and redecorating can cover a multitude of sins. Your self-esteem is on a self-destruct path. For perfect love is nature’s food to nourish a healthy self-esteem. Self-esteem is boosted or busted in an intimate relationship uncertainty of the outcome is a primary impediment to intimacy.

Intimacy is a God-created prescription to full-blown joy! The first reward of intimacy is a love that cures loneliness.

Loss of self-respect is too high a price to pay for any temporary pleasure. Love that flows free, pure and precious is life-giving. Love is a wellspring of enthusiasm! Optimism! Creativity! The joyous spring of love must never be allowed to run dry and must never, ever become cluttered with litter or polluted with foreign elements. A commitment will limit our individual development. Set goals, agree to “pay the price of self-sacrifice” resolve to solve problems learn to work through difficulties and not run away from them. That is positive prescription for emotional and mental maturity. This is an undebatable formula for “growing up” as a person.

The deeper the commitment the deeper the trust the deeper the freedom for both parties, without faith there is no freedom, without freedom there is no joy.

Life is a never ending process of collecting mostly happy memories.

How easy is the journey?

How light is the luggage?

A positive reaction is to accept the forgiveness that Jesus Christ offers and start a new life- here and now. Decide to live by a higher, safer more satisfactory moral standard!

Accept Christ’s forgiveness for the past. Then begin to improve your future and the future of society by setting higher standards than those which were set for you by a society skilled at self-deception and rationalization.

 

MAY GOD BLESS US ALL                                                         YOURS IN CHRIST SERVICE

Sujan Achen
