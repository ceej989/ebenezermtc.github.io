Greetings in the name of our Lord and Savior Jesus Christ. It is with great joy that we welcome you to Ebenezer Mar Thoma Church! Here, you will experience our deep passion for providing a meaningful place of worship, where our motto, “Lighted to Lighten,” truly comes to life. Our mission is to connect with God and grow in Christ through vibrant and powerful worship services.
